Competitions


From Regional Competitions, to the Provincial Championship, to the World Championship. LanceBotics has repeated this process 4 out of the 6 seasons we have competed, aiming to represent not only Toronto, but Canada as a whole. Each season, we compete at several Regional Competitions against other teams' robots in Ontario to qualify and prepare for the Provincial Championship. Since the 2016-2017 Starstruck season, all LanceBotics robot teams have qualified for the Ontario Provincial Championship every year -- and we aim to make this year no different from the past -- as 200X has already qualified through winning the Toronto VEX Competition! Below you may find a list of our upcoming competitions, as well as our results from past competitions.
