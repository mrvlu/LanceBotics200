Team 200G

200G is a VEX robotics team comprised solely of female members. The team was founded by Crystal J. and Sylvia G., as a result of a lack of female participation in robotics in the past. The idea was to encourage female participation in the male dominated field, and to share their passion for the STEM field with other girls.

The girls team has also garnered three awards this season, but fell short of advancing to the 2019 World Championship by two matches. Next season, their goal is to gather more female members from the Leaside community to show that girls do take part in STEM!
