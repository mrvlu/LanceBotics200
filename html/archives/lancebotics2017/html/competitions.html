2017 - 2018 VEX Robotics Competitions

LanceBotics 200 is a VEX EDR team that competes in VEX Robotics Competitions to put robots to the test, through code and through building. In comeptitions, we aim to not only win, but make friends along the way to make the competitions more interactive.

Here you may find a list of all the competitions we have attended thus far, along with https://vexdb.io/ team statistics links to our respective teams.


Past Competitions

Team Position W - L - T
200A 32nd 3 - 4 - 0
200B 49th 2 - 5 - 0
200C 39th (Quarterfinalist) 3 - 4 - 0

This is a Ontario Provincial Championship for VEX EDR. Teams from all over Ontario will compete to advance for the 2018 VEX Robotics World Championship in April.

Teams may also participate and advance through the highest Skills Challenge score by scoring the highest amount of points in driver skills and programming skills combined, each must be completed in a minute.
