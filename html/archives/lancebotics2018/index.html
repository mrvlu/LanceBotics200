Who are we?

LanceBotics is Leaside High School's VEX EDR Team that competes in the VEX Robotics Competition. Established in 2013, LanceBotics has never overlooked but maintained STEM education in every aspect of our team. In 4 Robotics Teams -- one of which is an all girls team -- we have many members who work hard to represent the Leaside community, their skills, and STEM. Our team has represented Toronto and Canada at the VEX Worlds Championship four times including this year, and each team aims to add another year to the counter.

We are the only team that represented Toronto at the 2019 VEX Robotics World Chamionship.
