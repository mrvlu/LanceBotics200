Virtual Worlds Team

Composed of a group of hard-working students in multiple grades, the Virtual Worlds Team is working towards creating a Robot simulation in a virtual world that can compete in a Robotics game. However, the team has higher hopes as they aim to qualify for Worlds with a top score in the virtual simulation.
