Team 200X

200X, the world qualifying team, consists of new and returning members, all with the goal of showing off their STEM skills and knowledge through VEX Robotics. Calling them a well oiled machine is an understatement. This year, they plan to not only advance to Freedom Hall, but to also take home the World Champion title.

200X is also founding member of the 6 Alliance, which is an international group of teams that collaborate and partner up in competitions. It allows members to connect with other STEM students all over the world for an ever more meaningful STEM experience. Out of the 20 teams in the 6 Alliance, 19 have qualified for the World Championship. The 6 Alliance has also collectively gathered over 140 awards this season alone. Click here for the 6 Alliance Instagram page.

200X is also the team that started up the VEX Ontario Teams Discord server, where teams from all over Ontario collaborate and talk about VEX, STEM, and other things robotics students would have in common. It was the first step made in order to becoming an internationally recognised team, which has turned out very successful.
