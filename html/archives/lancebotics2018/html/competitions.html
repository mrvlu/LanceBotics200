2018 - 2019 VEX Robotics Competitions

LanceBotics 200 is a VEX EDR team that competes in VEX Robotics Competitions to put robots to the test, through code and through building. In comeptitions, we aim to not only win, but make friends along the way to make the competitions more interactive.

Here you may find a list of all the competitions we shall attend or have attended thus far, along with https://vexdb.io/ team statistics links to our respective teams.

Past Competition

2019 VEX Robotics World Championships - April 24th to April 27th, 2019

Team Position (Math Division) W - L - T
200X 62nd 4 - 6 - 1

The VEX Robotics World Championship, presented by the Northrop Grumman Foundation, brings together top robotics teams in the VEX IQ Challenge, VEX Robotics Competition, and VEX U under one roof to celebrate their accomplishments and participate with the best teams from around the world.
