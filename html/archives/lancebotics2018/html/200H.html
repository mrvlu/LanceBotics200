Team 200H

200H is the Happy Hybrid three person team. These three members put a lot of effort into standing out among other teams, through special build choices and through making the impossible possible. Unfortunately, they did not qualify for the 2019 World Championship. Next year their goal is to advance to the international stage, all while standing out by not using common robot mechanisms on their robot.
