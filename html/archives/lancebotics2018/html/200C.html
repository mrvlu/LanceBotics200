Team 200C

200C is the grade 12 Computer Engineering class team, in their first year of robotics and most in their last year of high school. Although the season truly began after the course was over, their newfound passion for STEM made them a powerful team. The day after the course was complete was the tournament at which they gave themselves a name, going undefeated until the final match, where they would have easily won, if not for their alliance's robot disconnecting for the entire match. Unfortunately they did not advance to the 2019 World Championship, but they are not stopping there. Since it is their first and last opportunity to participate in the VEX Robotics Competition, they have merged with 200X in order to have a larger team with more resources to work with at the World Championship.
