Team 200G

200G is a VEX robotics team comprised solely of female members. 200G is an All-Girls VEX robotics team founded by Crystal J. and Sylvia G. After numerous years of competing in Vex Robotics, the two wanted to create an opportunity that encourages more girls to join and explore the male dominated STEM fields. Crystal and Sylvia were able to recruit and lead a team of 15 to design, build, and program Vex Robots.

Despite the fact that nearly all of 200G were rookies, the girls team garnered the Create Award at the Toronto VEX VRC tournament, the Judges Award at Mississauga VRC Qualifying Event, and the Build Award at the Ontario VRC Provincial Championships. Sadly, the team fell short of advancing to the 2019 World Championship by two matches. 200G ended their season being ranked 16th overall in Ontario for Robot skills. This is only the beginning of 200G and we hope to see even more success from them in the future.
