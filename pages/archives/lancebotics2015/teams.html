Team 200

LanceBotics Team 200 is an extracurricular robotics team of 45 Leaside High School students, a teacher volunteer, and parent helpers. LanceBotics Team 200 is divided into three different teams (Team 200A, 200B and 200C) competing against other schools in VEX Robotics competitions. By using robotics to apply our knowledge of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM), we maintain a fun and competitive environment. With the support of contributors and the Leaside community, LanceBotics will have many successful years to come.

Team Manager: Kohilan Mohanarajan
